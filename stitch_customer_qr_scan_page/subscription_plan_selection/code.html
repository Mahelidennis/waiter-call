Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later? expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes will be prorated for the current billing cycle.

What happens after my trial ends? expand_more

We will notify you before your 14-day trial ends. You can then choose to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using the service. If you don't, your account will be paused, but your data will not be deleted immediately.

Is there a setup fee? expand_more

No, there are no setup fees on any of our plans. You just pay the monthly or annual subscription fee. We also offer free onboarding assistance to get you started smoothly.
